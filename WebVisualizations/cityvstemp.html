Latitude vs Temperature



As seen in the plot, as the latitude gets closer to the equator the temperature also increases. In this example during July the temperature looks consistent between the southern and northern hemisphere, if not a little cooler in the southern hemisphere.

This is the summer season in the north and the winter season in the south.
